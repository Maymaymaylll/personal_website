Blue Echoes

An interactive electronic art installation. When viewers place their finger on the external pulse sensor, the whale model within the installation will begin to play sounds and move up and down, eventually synchronizing its motion frequency with the viewer's heartbeat. Through this installation, we aim to provoke thoughts on nature conservation and the coexistence of humans and nature.

My role: doing the programming and circuit designing work

Who wrote the script of my life

A participatory art piece. We invited individuals from various age groups and backgrounds to participate in interviews centered on the theme of "how external environments have influenced their past life choices." After the interviews, each participant was asked to draw a timeline of their lives based on the content discussed. Through this work, we aim to encourage both participants and viewers to reflect on how to navigate the relationship between their personal life choices and the influence of external factors.

My role: part of the interview conducting and video editing work
